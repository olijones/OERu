Summary of E-Activities

There are five designated E-Activities in this course. Learners seeking certification for active participation are required to submit three of their choice in addition to the microblog and forum post requirements.

We recommend that learners who are preparing to submit the course assignment for credit, complete all the E-Activities as these provide building blocks for your final assignment.

E-Activity Description
1st Learning reflection Blog post of 300 - 400 words reflecting on why open matters in education.
2nd Learning reflection Blog post of 300 - 400 words reflecting on defining open educational resources.
Activity 3.1 Developing two multiple choice questions (MCQs) based on your own mini copyright scenario or situation.
Activity 4.1 Blog post of 700 - 900 words based on practical example to reuse, revise, remix and redistribute Creative Commons resources plus reflection on the exercise.
3rd Learning reflection Blog post of 300 - 500 words reflecting on the choice of open license.
